About Me:

I'm a 20-year-old musician in my second year of studying Computer Science BSc at the University of Birmingham.

I started learning music at about five years old, after watching Queen's performance at Live Aid. I took piano and singing lessons for many years, getting up to Grade 5 in singing and Grade 7 in Piano. Additionally, I've been teaching myself ukulele since the start of the first lockdown.

At the moment:

I'm resting in a liminal emotional state, trying to keep up with my CS coursework and playing occasional open mics.
