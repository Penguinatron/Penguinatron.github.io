About

Hello, my name's Oliver and since April 2025 I have been working as an instrument technician with Hickies Music in Reading. The services I can provide include, but are not limited to:

  • Thorough cleaning and general maintenance
  • Restringing and setting up for common string instruments
  • Electronic repairs (guitar electronics, basic amplifier and keyboard repairs) and upgrades (new pickups, etcetera)

The instruments I can service include acoustic, electric and classical guitars, violins, violas and cellos, and limited work on keyboards, amplifiers and effect units. Feel free to contact me with any questions about specific repairs.

Learning

I am a junior technician at the shop, currently learning from and supervised by the previous incumbent, who worked in the role of instrument technician for around a decade. What I lack in long-term experience, I hope to make up for with a considered and careful approach, an aim to always improve my skills, and strong attention to the details.

Some services are currently beyond my ability to provide; if you have a potentially demanding repair, get in contact before you bring it to the shop to avoid disappointment.

Gadgets

As well as the above repair services, I have also designed several (mostly MIDI-based) instruments and accessories, which I can build on-demand and to specifications. Feel free to contact me if you're interested in any of these.

Repairs Pricing

For repairs conducted via Hickies, labour prices are as follows:

Service Details Starting Cost (labour only)
Restring Includes a full check-over and clean, fret polishing, fretboard conditioning. £25
Setup Neck relief, nut, saddles and bridge are adjusted for the best possible action and intonation, making the guitar feel better to play. Includes a restring as described above. £50
Electronics Silent, buzzing or scratchy jacks, pots, and pickups can be cleaned, fixed, reconnected or replaced as necessary. Usually necessitates a restring as the strings often need to come off to fully access the electronics. £40/hr
Small Repairs Any little problems that need putting right; broken tuners, strap button installation, minor adjustments, single string replacements. These can often be done on the spot if I'm in the shop, and are charged according to the problem at hand. £5-15

Any necessary parts (strings, etc.) will be charged at cost price to me, including shipping when applicable. The prices above are guides; any substantial labour price increases will be checked with you first before work is carried out.
